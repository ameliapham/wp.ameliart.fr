About Amelia

Hello,
Glad you’re here. A little about me...

I am a graduate of the University of Sciences, Socials, and Humanities of Hanoi (Vietnam) where I earned my licence of International Studies. I have 1.5 years of work experience as a social media marketer for Kinergie Studio and 5 years of freelance experience in design. After finishing my study at the University Paul Valery of Montpellier 3 with the aim of improving my French, I’m currently studying in Management at the School of Management IAE Lyon. This portfolio is about my professional experiences, the activities I have taken part in, projects I have led and on a more casual note on my way of seeing the world.
